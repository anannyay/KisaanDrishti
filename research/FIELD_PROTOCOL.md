# CropPulse field data protocol

## Minimum defensible pilot

- 30 individually identified plants from at least three plots
- 8–12 observations per plant over four or more weeks
- At least two phone models and multiple operators
- Healthy controls retained in every plot
- Plant ID card, scale marker and colour reference in calibration images
- Expert health score recorded without seeing the algorithm output

## Capture sequence

1. Scan the same plant ID.
2. Capture the whole canopy from a repeatable distance and direction.
3. Capture one closer view of representative leaves.
4. Record growth stage, recent intervention and unusual weather.
5. Do not remove failed images; mark the reason for failure.

## Ethics and claims

Obtain farm permission and avoid exact public coordinates. CropPulse measurements are research observations, not treatment prescriptions. Hold out entire plants, plots and phones during evaluation; never randomly split adjacent photographs from one plant across train and test sets.
